I am a third year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science at UT Austin, advised by Professor Daehyeok Kim, where I am a part of the UTNS Research Group (University of Texas Networked Systems).

My research focuses on Computer Systems, specifically in the intersection of Operating Systems and Machine Learning. Recently, I've been working on identifying the inefficiencies of CPU Schedulers (especially CFS of Linux), and how we can leverage Machine Learning to help the Operating System make optimal decisions under different environments.
